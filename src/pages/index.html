OUR STORY

Brighten Up My Smile formed out of my own personal story to find the right ritual that worked for me in order to keep my wisdom teeth. Thus, FlossStonesTM was created!

Being a life-long entreprenuer I was inspired to create these beautiful little stones for others that encouraged them to form healthy habits that actually work.

My vision is to help support humanitarian projects through my products while also delivering thoughtful and heart-warming gifts that can aid everyone form heart healthy habits. Thank you visiting Brightenupmysmile.com!
